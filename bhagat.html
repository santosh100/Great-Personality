BHAGAT SINGH (1907-1931)
Name- Bhagat Singh
Born- 28 sep 1907 (Banga,Pakistan)
Died- 23 mar 1931 (Lahore,Pakistan)
Education- Commity National College Lahore
Parent- Sardar Kishan Singh Sandhu(Father),Vidyavati(Mother)
Founded- Navjawan Bharat Sabha

Bhagat Singh was one of the most well known Indian freedom fighters and Indian revolutionaries and who contributed significantly in the indian freedom strugle against the British. Bhagat Singh bonrn in a family that was activily involved in revolutionary activities against the British rule in the country. He is also addressed as SAHID BHAGAT SINGH. He was prominent member of various revolutionary associations and was also one of the major leaders of the Hindustan Republican Association.
