A.P.J. ABDUL KALAM (1931-2015)
Name- Avul Pakir Jainulabudeen Abdul Kalam
Norn- 15 october 1931(Rameshwaram)
Died- 27 july 2015 (Shillong)
Education- Madras Institute of Technology(1955-1960)
Parent- Jainulabudeen Marakayar(father),Ashiamma Jainulabudeen(Mother)
Award- Bharat Ratnma(1997),Padma Vibhushan(1990),Padma Bhushan(1981)

Kalam was a scientist and engineer, who served as the president of india from 2002-07. Already a highly accomplished and much respected individual when elected to be the president. Kalam had spent decades as scientist and science administrator at several prestitious organization like the defence research and development organization and Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO). His initial dream was to become a fighter pilot but he failed to qualify for the INDIAN AIRFORCE. The renowned scientist who was fondly called the Missile Man.
