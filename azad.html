CHANDRA SHEKHAR AZAD (1906-1931)
Name - Chandra Shekhar Azad
Born- 23 july 1906 (Bhavra,India)
Died- 27 feb 1931 (Allahabad,India)
Education- Mahatma Gandi Kashi Vidyapith
Parent- Sitaram Tiwari(Father),Jagrani Devi(Mother)
Movement- Indian independence movement

Azad was a great Indian freedom fighter. He fierce patriatism and courage inspired other of his generation to enter freedom struggle. Azad was the mentor Bhagat singh, another great freedom fighter and along with Bhagat Singh. He is considered as one of the greatest revolutionaries that India has produced. In 1921 when Mahatam Gandhi launched Non Co-operation movement Azad activily participated in revolutionary activities. After the suspension of non-co-operation movement Azad attracted towards more aggrassive and revolutionary ideals. Azad was a terror for British police.
